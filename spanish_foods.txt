pozole - spicy mexican soup with pork, radish, cabbage
cochinita pibil - yucatecan pork in achiote and citrus, slow-roasted in banana leaves
chile relleno - cheese-stuffed poblano, egg-battered and fried, often in tomato salsa

sincronizadas - two flour tortillas with ham and melted cheese, griddled flat like a pressed quesadilla
enchiladas - corn tortillas dipped in salsa, rolled around filling, baked with sauce and cheese on top
tacos a la diabla - tacos of meat in a very spicy red chile sauce, often shrimp or beef, seriously hot
quesadilla rellena - thick folded or stuffed quesadilla packed with cheese plus meat, mushrooms, or other fillings
mole poblano - rich dark sauce from puebla with chiles, seeds, spices, and a hint of chocolate, over poultry
enchiladas verdes - enchiladas sauced with tart green tomatillo-serrano salsa, often chicken, crema, and cheese
tacos de birria - tacos of slow-braised spiced meat (often beef or goat) with consomé for dipping
carnitas - pork shoulder simmered until tender then crisped in its own fat, shredded for tacos with onion and cilantro
